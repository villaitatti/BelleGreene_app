[p. 3]

I plan to leave here for Dresden on the 8th – and to leave Munich on the 17th – enroute to B.B. (foolish people call it Salzburg)

I had to leave the Esplanade as I stupidly had not told them how long I wanted to stay when I engaged the rooms from New York –

By the grace of God – this delightful apartment was free as Goldschmidt is on his way to U.S.A – to receive a degree at the Harvard Ter –etc. I am so much happier here – A sort of lady-housekeeper, and an excellent servant (I would pay her 4 times – if I could get her) – [double underlined: all] the Art Ref books in the world – make for every comfort – I am enjoying Berlin enormously – Crowdsare pouring in, and by Saturday I dare say the place will be a madhouse & for a week after I leave – but I will like that also – for it was like a Tomb when I arrived.

Bitte – R.S.V.P. – P.D.L – Belle
