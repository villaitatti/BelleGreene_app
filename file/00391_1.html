Friday, July 2 – 1915

Dearest B. B.

I have just received your letter of June 14th from Fiuggi – a very interesting letter by the way – In it you speak of a cable sent me evidently a month previous – I have never received it – of course I amso cross that you are quite happy in Italy and have no desire to come over here – Does that mean you are not coming in the fall? I had
