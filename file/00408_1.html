Tuesday, Sept. 19 – 1916

Dearest B. B. –

I have just received yours of August 29th, which in view of your cable is of course null & void – needless to say I am impatiently awaiting you letter of explanation –

I do wish you did not lay so much stress upon correspondence as everyone knows I am the worst one in the world co.
