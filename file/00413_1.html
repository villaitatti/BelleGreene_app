Sunday, 7 p.m.

Dearest B. B. –

I have just cabled you of my arrival here – you cannot imagine how awfully queer it feels to be here without you – I simply cannot get used to it – we had a horrid passage – the ship (American Line) was a horrid little tub and when it was not

[p. 2]

standing on both ends or rolling from side to side, it was swiftly sinking in the centre – Do you know the kind?
