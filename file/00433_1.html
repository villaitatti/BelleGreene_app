Sept. 18. 1917

Dear B.B. –

Indeed I was delighted to receive your letter from Vallombrosa – It is possible that a place

with so heavenly a name still exists in this bedevilled world! Ever since I returned from London I have been head over heels in war work of one kind or another – I was immensely privileged early in the year to be given some rather important work to do but of course I cannot write anything of that – When that finished (and successfully thank God) I went into the Red Cross which has been reorganized with Mr. Davison (Mr. Morgan's partner) at the head.
