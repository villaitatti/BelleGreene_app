The man wears his wedding ring on his head & the woman on her breast – most interesting & a bit futuristic –

I can hardly write on this vile train – don't know how you are going to read it – what a dreadful time it takes for letters to go to & from Paris – How I wish I were going to have a few weeks there – a friend of mine came over from there last week

[p. 16]

and he says they are all very optimistic & think the war will be over by Spring – but not I – Now I really must stop as this trying to hold down the paper in this rickety rackety train is driving me mad – All my love to you dearest – I will write again next Monday – You can write to me at Claridges up to the 19th – After that S.S St. Louis Liverpool – sailing Nov. 25

Devotedly yours,

Belle
