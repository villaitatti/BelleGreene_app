Nov. 20

Monday

Dearest B.B –

I got no further than the enclosed at an attempt to you while week-ending at the Holford’s & will try to write now before I must dress for dinner with the Morgans – I had a most delightful time at the Holfords although one were only a small party (in that [double underlined: huge] house with its 16,000 + acreage!) as Lord Grey was ill & Lord & Lady Crawford were caught somewhere in the storm – We tramped in the snow & motored to tea to Badminton

[p. 2]

& some “titles” came in for dinner – I saw all the pictures & was quitemad about a few of them, notably 2 full length Van Dycks, 2 Titian’s, a small profile head of a girl with light blond hair tightly pulled back which looked like a Domenico Veneziano to me, 2 fine Rembrandts (a self portrait & one of his mother) a splendid Rubens sketch, a fine small Perugino (???) and the [double underlined: finest] Cuyp I have ever seen although I do not much care for Cuyp in any condition.
