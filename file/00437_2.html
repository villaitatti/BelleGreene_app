well be in an Island in the Pacific, as within 2 1/2 hours of New York – There is nothing but water and a lighthouse to be seen and not a human but my sister and the servants – all of whom keep miles away from me – this scrawl is being made on the beach with some vile brownish fluid called ink– so scuse please –

Of course I'm quite crazy about him – not at all on account of this looks – which is what attracts everyone else – but because of his truly amazing intelligence – Its almost uncanny and sometimes he does make me fearful
