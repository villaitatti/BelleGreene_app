My last numbered days in Paris were madly hectic and I had to leave numberless things undone – of course I broke all my social engagements upon news of Mrs. Morgan’s death and confined my ‘eating’ times to one or two men whom I know very well, and to such uninhabited places as Ville d’Array etc.
