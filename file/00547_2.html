head waiter & all here are aghast, as it was the first news they had had of conditions as they really were on the boat.

Were it not that I must be back in N.Y. on the 1st Oct., I would stay here too.

Personally, I have no right to complain, as I calmly + brazenly threw the fear of J. P. into them & they have given me a suite de gran lusso of bedroom sitting room & large bath but the others are suffering badly. It will all be a boomerang for themforall the newspapers will have the story shortly after our arrival. How I miss bothyoudears. B.G.
