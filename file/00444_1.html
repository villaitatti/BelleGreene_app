[p. 1]

[In pencil: Feb. 11

1920]

Dear B.B –

This is only a horrid scrawl to say that I have had to postpone my sailing until the end of February or early in March –

My mother and sister now have taken very suddenly ill – mother with evidences of Typhoid (they are not certain of it at this writing) and my sister has just undergone a most serious operation (yesterday) we do not know yet if she is out of danger – So you can see I could not think of leaving –

Do not plan to meet me in Rome on my first trip there
