On Train

Paris to Cherbourg

May 12, 1920,

Dear B.B –

I am sorry that your letter of May 5, with introduction to Fröhnercame only on the morning of the 10th I had not a single free minute that day and yesterday was laid up in bed all day with some sort of cold in the jaw!!

Whatever the damned thing is, my right

cheek is swollen to twice its size and I am a sight to behold. When I arrived in Paris, I found the Ritz had taken a room for me in this hotel, where the food is good but everything else villainous. Had great excitement this morning as they agreed to cart me and my hand bags to the station, in consequence of which promise I had refused the offer of four private autos
