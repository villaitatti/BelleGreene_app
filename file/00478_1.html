Friday, 8 April, 1921

Dear B.B. –

Every moment since my last writing has been more than fully occupied – largely with people or matters of little [^no^] interest to you –

I keep increasingly well and, probably in consequence, increasingly happy in an [^impersonally^] joyous interested way – It may probably be due to the unusually glorious weather we have been having – It keeps me on steadily buoyant tiptoe.

I have gone to see René
