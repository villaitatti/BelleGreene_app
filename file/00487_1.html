25 April [^May^], 1921

Dear B. B. –

I found your letters of April 28, 30 and May 2 – awaiting me on my return. They could not have arrived before the 21st. (3 weeks en route!) as all personal mail was forwarded to me

[p. 2]

up to that date. My new cook does it from the from the house. She writes an 'elegant' Spencerian hand. & in this style – "Madam, I send the letters that look right. We consume less than when you are here – I am to have a gentleman for dinner tonight but provide for him out of my own pocket book. I'll attend to it
