He asked for pictures of our Library and I promised them, my reports and one or two little things – and he gave me a rosary! – Then as I was leaving (I was tickled pink when he came downstairs with me and we passed that old devil at the barred window) he said “We would never treat with anyone except M. Pierpont Morgan or with you as his representative – and if you will send me a letter, I will take it up with the Order” – It was all I could do to keep a straight face. I learned later that he has only been Erzabt for a few years – that he is a business-genius and for that reason particularly, was chosen to replace the former incumbent who evidently got into difficulties
