Washington & Chicago for 2 days each – This will be the extent of my wanderings until the summer, unless I have to go to Rome (which Gawd forbid) in the Spring –

There does not seem to be any news except that we all are more & more financially depressed. I hear that Boston, on the contrary, is quite calm & smiling and looking into the future unabashed –

The banks are becoming definite about "cashing in" on loans and the some commercial houses talk e of shutting up shop for a time –
