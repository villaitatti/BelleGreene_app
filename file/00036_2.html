I did after all count for something in your life – and that is the greatest flattery to a woman – I heard of Lady Sassoon’s death while at Newport and felt for you so keenly – How I longed to be with you – to comfort you all I might in my small way – Alas! it were vain to hope that I might fill either one of their places especially your friend Rembielinski (What an enchanting name!)

[p. 4]

I have neither the intellect, the knowledge of things and life, or the large grasp he had – This I realize to a sufferable degree – all that I can bring to you is my real love for you – a love that would take you up and hold you close were you millions of miles away – and in such a moment as this kiss and caress you into a forgetfulness of the pain – or at last nullify it
