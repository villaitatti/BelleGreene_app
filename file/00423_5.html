I am spending Sunday with the Evans’ and will write all about their collection on Monday –

I’m haggard and worn from lack of sleep although I [double underlined: hate] to leave here – will be glad to get on the steamer &sleep all the 7 days – I am now sailing on the St. Louis from Liverpool on the 25th (Saturday) & expect

[p. 8]

to reach N.Y. on Dec. 3rd – How I shall miss all this – Somehow they seem to think a Librarian is quite a personage here – & it is quite awful for they expect me to know everything – Often it is easy as I find the collectors themselves in most instances know nothing – Indeed Mrs. Jack M. is more intelligent than almost any woman (excepting [double underlined: always] Mrs. Benson) that I have met here.
