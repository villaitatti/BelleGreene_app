Monday Nov. 6

Dearest B.B. –

I have this morning received your letters of Oct. 28&31 – From my letter of yesterday you will see that I know only too well the difficulties of crossing and returning and evidently I knowmoreof the reasons thanyou do – at least as evinced by your letters&that they will continue for some time –My Chiefhas absolutely put his

[p. 2]

foot down on my goingback in any but an American boat& if you come over in August I strongly give you the same advice. It is not safe. You will receive my telegrambefore this telling you I have had to definitely give up the idea of getting to Paris
