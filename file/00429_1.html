Saturday Nov.

Dearest B.B –

First of all it is snowing lightly & freezing every morn except in this house and it makes everything look too beautiful for words – You probably are used to seeing deep green grass & trees full of yellow and red leaves with niches of snow upon the ground but I am not & it simply intoxicates me – And I am enamoured of the Holfords which further

[p. 2]
