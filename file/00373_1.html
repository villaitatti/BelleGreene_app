Wednesday 11 p.m.

Dear B. B. -

Would it bother you very much to write a letter to Eugene Meyer (jr.) giving him your opinion of the Educational value and importance of the Freer collection? He is so turribly keen on getting a degree from Yale for Freer and of course, a (nice) letter

[p.2]

from you would be so valuable –

Any time within the next week would do – perhaps you would find a moment at Northampton in which to compose it –

But – please "me dearling" don't do it – unless you feel that it is fully justified –

I am just "beating it" for me Virgin
