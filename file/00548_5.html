[p. 6]

[Written longways]

My loving affection to the Nicky- B.B. combine – May your dear shadows never separate or grow less –

B.G.

[The following written in pencil]: Saturday, 8am

P.S – We are just arriving amid a hellaballoo of Italian excitement and as the boat ought to be laid up for 2 weeks to have her plumbing fixed, I’ll keep this to send by the Aquitania –
