B.B.B.

As I am never quite certain (as we do not speak the same language!) whether the servant here has posted letters or not – I write to confirm (or to acquaint you with!) my last letter – to the effect that I have arranged to reach Salzburg on the 17th surely – and possibly on the 16th. I shall stay at, what I am given to understand, is a second rate hostelry – but the only one which could accommodate me, the Habsburg Hof

If you ever get this do let me hear that we are really going to meet again before I am completely senile (I am what is called gaga already)
