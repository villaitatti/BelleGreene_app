He has made a splendid impression upon the entire city and I think has done more to quell the "Irish" feeling & any anti-English feeling here – than all the hundreds of propogandists etc that have come over during & after the War – The people actually took him right into their hearts and I honestly believe that very few, expect as ceremony required, thought of him at all as the next king of England, but simply as an extremely well bred – extremely young & extremely lovable representation of England –
