Dearest B.B. –

Since writing you last my plans have crystallized and I leave here on the 11th for Paris – and from there on the 13th for Rome – I believe I am due there some time around midnight on the 14th.

I have wired the Excelsior for rooms but cannot be sure of anything

[ Written on the top margin, sideways : Just reread your letter of the 23rd. Lordy what biting sarcasm . "Our BEST & Most CONSERVATIVE facilities." Poor little B.G. [ double underlined : Do] I get a Pesellino if I come to Settignano & weep on Mary's shoulder?]

[p. 2]

– I must get on the job in Rome first and foremost, and I honestly cannot tell from here how long it will take me to look things over and perhaps straighten things out –

Now please do whatever is best for your health and comfort – If you cannot get to Rome the I will manage somehow to get to I Tatti if only for a weekend –
